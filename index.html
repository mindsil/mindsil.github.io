Frequently Asked Questions

Is Navyug Educare truly 100% free?
Yes, absolutely! All our services including career counselling, admission guidance, and placement support are completely free. We believe quality education consulting should be accessible to every deserving student without any financial burden.
Who is eligible for your services?
Our services are open to students of all academic backgrounds - whether you're in 10th, 12th, pursuing your bachelor's degree, or considering postgraduate studies. We also support working professionals looking to upskill or transition careers.
What is the career counselling process like?
Our process includes personality assessment tests, aptitude evaluations, one-on-one consultations with expert counselors, and personalized career roadmap creation. Sessions are typically conducted online via video calls or at our Delhi and Rohtak offices for in-person meetings.
How do you help with university admissions?
We provide comprehensive admission support including university shortlisting based on your profile, application strategy development, statement of purpose (SOP) writing assistance, entrance exam preparation guidance, and interview coaching to maximize your chances of acceptance.
Where are your offices located?
We have offices in Delhi and Rohtak. However, most of our services are conducted online, so you can access our counselors from anywhere in India. You can also visit our physical offices for in-person consultations by appointment.
How can I book a consultation?
Simply fill out the consultation form on this page with your details, or reach out to us via WhatsApp at +91-9876543210 or email at info@navyugeducareindia.com. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule your session.
Do you help with international university admissions?
Yes! We provide guidance for admissions to universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and other countries. Our counselors help with application strategies, test preparation (GMAT, GRE, TOEFL), and visa guidance.
What placement services do you offer?
Our placement services include resume optimization, LinkedIn profile building, interview preparation, technical skill coaching, soft skills training, and direct connections with leading companies for internships and full-time opportunities.
